## Overview

The Subscription Management system handles billing for platform access. Key features:

- **Flexible Plans:** Monthly, quarterly, and yearly billing cycles
- **Free Trials:** Try before you commit
- **Auto-Renewal:** Seamless subscription continuation
- **Invoice Management:** View and download all invoices
- **Multiple Payment Options:** Pay with mobile wallets or cards
- **Reminder Notifications:** Never miss a renewal date

## Getting Started

### What is a Subscription?

A subscription is your platform access license. It determines:

- Number of customers you can manage
- Number of sub-operators you can create
- Number of routers you can connect
- Access to premium features
- Support level

### Prerequisites

- Registered operator account
- Valid tenant/organization setup
- Payment method available

### Accessing Subscription Management

1. Log in to your operator panel
2. Navigate to **Subscriptions** from the sidebar
3. View available plans and your current subscription

## Subscribing to a Plan

### New Subscription

#### Step 1: Choose Your Plan

1. Navigate to **Subscriptions** â†’ **Plans**
2. Review plan features and pricing
3. Click **"View Details"** for more information
4. Click **"Subscribe Now"** on your chosen plan

#### Step 2: Review Plan Details

On the plan details page, you'll see:

- Complete feature list
- Pricing breakdown
- Billing cycle options
- Trial period (if applicable)
- Usage limits

#### Step 3: Confirm Subscription

1. Review the subscription terms
2. Click **"Subscribe Now"**
3. Confirm your choice in the popup
4. System creates your subscription

#### Step 4: Trial Period (if applicable)

- Subscription starts immediately in trial mode
- No payment required during trial
- Full access to all plan features
- Reminder sent 3 days before trial ends

#### Step 5: First Payment

After trial (or immediately for no-trial plans):

1. Receive payment due notification
2. Click **"Pay Now"** in email or dashboard
3. Choose payment method
4. Complete payment
5. Subscription activated

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Current Subscription

**Dashboard View:**
1. Your current plan is displayed on the dashboard
2. Shows expiration date and status
3. Quick access to renewal

**Detailed View:**
1. Navigate to **Subscriptions** â†’ **My Subscription**
2. View complete subscription details:
   - Plan name and features
   - Start and end dates
   - Billing cycle
   - Usage statistics
   - Next billing date
   - Payment history

### Subscription Status

Your subscription can have different statuses:

- **Trial:** Free trial period, full access
- **Active:** Paid and current, full access
- **Expiring Soon:** Less than 7 days remaining
- **Grace Period:** 3 days past due, limited access
- **Suspended:** Payment overdue, limited access
- **Cancelled:** Subscription terminated

### Upgrading Your Plan

**When to upgrade:**
- Approaching customer limit
- Need more sub-operators
- Want additional features
- Require better support

**How to upgrade:**
1. Go to **Subscriptions** â†’ **Plans**
2. Select a higher-tier plan
3. Click **"Upgrade Now"**
4. Pay the pro-rated difference
5. Upgrade takes effect immediately

**Pro-rated billing:**
- You only pay for the remaining time
- Unused time from old plan is credited
- New plan starts immediately

### Downgrading Your Plan

**Important:** Downgrades take effect at next renewal

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Subscriptions** â†’ **My Subscription**
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select lower-tier plan
4. Confirm downgrade
5. Continue using current plan until renewal

**Note:** Ensure your usage fits within new plan limits before downgrading

## Billing and Invoices

### Understanding Bills

**Bill Generation:**
- Monthly plans: Billed on the same date each month
- Quarterly plans: Every 3 months
- Yearly plans: Once per year

**Bill Components:**
- Subscription fee
- Pro-rated adjustments (if any)
- Taxes (if applicable)
- Total amount due

**Due Date:**
- Usually 7 days after bill generation
- Grace period: 3 days after due date
- Late fee may apply after grace period

### Viewing Invoices

**Access invoices:**
1. Navigate to **Subscriptions** â†’ **Bills & Invoices**
2. View all historical invoices
3. Filter by status or date

**Invoice details include:**
- Invoice number
- Billing period
- Amount charged
- Payment date
- Payment method
- Download PDF option

### Downloading Invoices

**For paid bills:**
1. Go to **Subscriptions** â†’ **Bills & Invoices**
2. Find the invoice you need
3. Click **"Download PDF"**
4. Save to your computer

**Uses for invoices:**
- Accounting records
- Tax filing
- Expense reports
- Business documentation

### Payment History

View complete payment history:

1. **Subscriptions** â†’ **Bills & Invoices**
2. See all transactions
3. Filter by date, status, or amount
4. Export to CSV for accounting

## Renewal and Cancellation

### Automatic Renewal

**How it works:**
- Subscriptions auto-renew by default
- Bill generated before expiration
- Payment reminder sent 7 days prior
- Auto-payment if payment method saved
- Manual payment if needed

**Renewal reminders:**
- 7 days before: First reminder
- 3 days before: Second reminder
- 1 day before: Final reminder
- On due date: Payment due notice

### Manual Renewal

**If auto-renewal fails:**
1. Check your email for payment link
2. Or go to **Subscriptions** â†’ **Bills**
3. Click **"Pay Now"** on pending bill
4. Complete payment
5. Subscription renewed immediately

### Cancelling Subscription

**Before you cancel:**
- Download all your data
- Export customer information
- Save important reports
- Note: Data may be deleted after 30 days

**Cancellation process:**
1. Go to **Subscriptions** â†’ **My Subscription**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Select cancellation reason
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Access continues until period end

**What happens:**
- No more billing
- Access until current period ends
- No refunds for unused time
- Data retained for 30 days
- Can resubscribe anytime

### Reactivating Cancelled Subscription

**Within 30 days:**
1. Go to **Subscriptions** â†’ **Plans**
2. Choose your previous plan (or different one)
3. Subscribe again
4. Your data is restored

**After 30 days:**
- Data is permanently deleted
- Must start fresh
- Cannot recover old data

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Issues

**Problem: Payment failed**

**Solutions:**
1. Verify payment method has sufficient funds
2. Check card expiration date
3. Ensure billing address is correct
4. Try a different payment method
5. Contact your payment provider
6. Contact support with error message

**Problem: Charged twice**

**Solutions:**
1. Check if both charges are from our system
2. One might be a pre-authorization (refunded automatically)
3. Contact support with transaction IDs
4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

**Problem: Invoice not generated**

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 24 hours after billing date
2. Check spam folder for invoice email
3. Manually download from Bills section
4. Contact support if still missing

### Subscription Issues

**Problem: Cannot upgrade/downgrade**

**Solutions:**
1. Check if there's a pending payment
2. Ensure you're not in trial period
3. Verify plan availability
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try different browser
6. Contact support

**Problem: Subscription shows as expired but you paid**

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 5-10 minutes for system update
2. Refresh the page
3. Check payment was actually completed
4. Verify transaction ID
5. Contact support with proof of payment

**Problem: Features limited despite active subscription**

**Solutions:**
1. Check subscription status
2. Verify payment is not overdue
3. Ensure plan includes the features
4. Log out and log back in
5. Clear cache and cookies
6. Contact support

### Access Issues

**Problem: Account suspended**

**Reasons:**
- Payment overdue
- Terms violation
- Security issue

**Solutions:**
1. Check for overdue payments
2. Make payment if applicable
3. Contact support for other issues
4. Follow reactivation process

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Can I change plans mid-cycle?**  
A: Yes, upgrades apply immediately with pro-rated billing. Downgrades take effect at renewal.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for long-term commitments?**  
A: Yes, save 10% on quarterly and 15-20% on yearly plans.

**Q: What happens if I exceed plan limits?**  
A: You'll receive notifications. Upgrade prompt appears, or some features may be limited.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**  
A: No, but you can cancel and resubscribe later. Data retained for 30 days.

### Billing Questions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: bKash, Nagad, Rocket, SSLCommerz (Visa, Mastercard, Amex).

**Q: Are there setup fees?**  
A: No, only the subscription fee. No hidden charges.

**Q: Do prices include taxes?**  
A: Prices are shown before tax. Applicable taxes added at checkout.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Generally no refunds for subscription services. Contact support for special cases.

### Trial Questions

**Q: Do I need a credit card for the trial?**  
A: No credit card required for trial period.

**Q: What happens when trial ends?**  
A: You'll be prompted to enter payment information to continue.

**Q: Can I cancel during trial?**  
A: Yes, cancel anytime during trial with no charges.

**Q: Can I get another trial?**  
A: Trials are one-time per organization.
